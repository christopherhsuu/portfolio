Batter Archetypes

An exploratory clustering of hitters to identify archetypes (power/speed/contact/etc.) and visualize archetype profiles.

Open Live App Repository

Methodology

I aggregated seasonal batting metrics and selected features (ISO, K%, BB%, Sprint Speed, exit velocity percentiles). After scaling, I applied k-means and silhouette analysis to choose cluster counts and produced archetype centroids for interpretation.

Findings

The clusters reveal intuitive groups: power hitters with high ISO and exit velocity, contact specialists with low K% and high batting average, and speed-oriented hitters. The visualization helps scouting and lineup construction experiments.

How I built it

  1. Data ingest: batting metrics + Statcast for speed and exit velocity.
  2. Feature engineering: percentiles and rate metrics; imputed missing values for players with limited playing time.
  3. Clustering: k-means and silhouette analysis; centroid visualization.
  4. Deployment: Streamlit app to explore clusters and player membership.
