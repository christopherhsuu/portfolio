About

I’m a first-year Data Science student at Boston University with a focus on applying data-driven methods to sports, especially baseball. I’m passionate about using machine learning, predictive modeling, and exploratory analysis to uncover insights about player performance and game strategy. Whether I’m analyzing Statcast data or building models to predict outcomes, I enjoy transforming raw data into stories that deepen our understanding of the game.

Background

I work with reproducible notebooks, interactive visualizations, and statistical models to explore player performance and game strategy. My projects focus on transparency and reproducibility so others can build on the work.
